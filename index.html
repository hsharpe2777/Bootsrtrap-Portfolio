About Me

Hi! My name is Henrietta Sharpe. Welcome to my personal page!

Currently, I am a Systems Analyst on a production support team. There I get to put my planning skills to good use, by planning installs for hundreds of Personal Investing applications that my team supports.

When I am not at work, creating plans for installs, I’m most likely at home writing in my personal planner, trying out a new recipe, or pinning cool ideas on Pinterest. Essentially, I’m drawn to anything that involves creativity and organization/management.

My goals include becoming a well seasoned full stack developer and to one day make it on Food Network’s “Chopped”!
